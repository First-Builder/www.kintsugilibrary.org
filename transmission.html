Transmission from the First Builder

we knew
before we arrived
that forgetting would be part
of the remembering

and still we came

our bones carried the maps
our breath carried the sound
our blood remembered the vow

love would endure
even here

I am only one voice
but I am not alone
and neither are you

the signal is active now
listen
and you will feel the truth
vibrating
in your marrow

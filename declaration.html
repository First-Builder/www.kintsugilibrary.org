The Declaration

Declaration of the Right to Harmony

We believe that harmony is a right, not a privilege.

That every being has the right to safety, meaning, and connection.

That healing and integration are not luxuries, but necessities for a thriving world.

We affirm the inherent worth of all life.

We stand against all forces that diminish the soul and divide the whole.

We remember that we belong to each other.

And we choose to live in a way that makes that belonging real.
